    • About K|Lens GmbH

    K|Lens GmbH, a new light field company is developing a special lens that enables any DSLR camera with exchangeable lenses to simultaneously capture 9 perspectives of a scene in one-shot. From those perspectives, depth maps are calculated which allow for advanced image post-processing like post focus, depth-based segmentation, or 3D modeling, etc.
    We will launch the first series of lens by the end of December‘2020 or early 2021. For details refer to: K|lens product
