    • Bio

    I am currently working as a Computer Vision Researcher in K|Lens, GmbH, Germany. I am interested in 3D Computer Vision, Deep-Learning & Image quality assessment.

    Prior to this, I obtained my Ph.D. in The Hong Kong University of Science & Technology (HKUST) in 2017. During my Ph.D., I was a visiting research scholar in the VAI lab at Stony Brook University, USA and SUNY Korea in 2015/2016. I also visited Technicolor R & D, France as a research internee and awarded "Best Research and Innovation Award" of 2017. In 2012, I received an undergraduate degree from LNMIIT, India in the ECE department.



    About K|Lens GmbH

    K|Lens GmbH, a new light field company is developing a special lens that enables any DSLR camera with exchangeable lenses to simultaneously capture 9 perspectives of a scene in one-shot. From those perspectives, depth maps are calculated which allow for advanced image post-processing like post focus, depth-based segmentation, or 3D modeling, etc.
